# Healthcare And PHI Rules

- Treat healthcare data as PHI unless clearly synthetic.
- Use minimum necessary data for workflow work.
- Mask patient names, member IDs, dates of birth, addresses, claim numbers, and payer identifiers in examples.
- Do not provide diagnosis, treatment advice, legal advice, or final payer determinations.
- Do not transmit PHI to unapproved systems or include PHI in prompts, logs, screenshots, telemetry, or fixtures.
